Medium Rare Steak

The Steak You Will Ever Make Yourself

This will be the best steak you have ever dreamed of making yourself. Perfectly medium rare with a juicy garlic butter on top.

Ingredients

  • Ribeye steak
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Salt and pepper the steak
  2. Bring stove to a high heat then add oil to pan
  3. Cook steak for 3 minutes then flip
  4. Put garlic and butter in pan and begin to baste steak
  5. After 3 minutes place steak on its side and cook each side for 30 seconds
  6. Take steak out of pan and let rest for 10 minutes
