Pasta

The Best Pasta In The Galaxy

This pasta will make you never have another pasta in your life. The creamy sauce with the crispy chicken is like nothing you have had.

Ingredients

  • Penne Pasta
  • Chicken Breast
  • Tomato Paste
  • Heavy Cream
  • Garlic
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Boil pasta until tender
  2. Season and cook chicken breast on stove until done
  3. Saute onions and garlic in pan
  4. Once onions and garlic are tender add tomato paste
  5. Afte tomato paste is cooked add heavy cream
  6. add pasta water then let simmer for 5 minutes
  7. Mix everything together and serve
