Garlic Mashed Potatoes

The Creamiest Garlic Mashed Potatoes You Can Makes

These mashed potatoes will make you never go out to eat again. Easy to make and so delicious you'll be sharing the recipe with everyone you know.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salt And Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil potatoes until soft
  2. Smash the potatoes and add salt and pepper to taste
  3. Add one table spoon of butter
  4. Add half cup of heavy cream
  5. Add two cloves of minced garlic
  6. Mix and let simmer while stirring for three minutes
  7. Serve
